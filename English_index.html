About Elegant Hospitality

Elegant is a leader in real estate technology and the future of the hospitality sector in the Middle East. We offer luxurious accommodation experiences in prime locations across Egypt, Saudi Arabia, and Dubai. By leveraging advanced technology, we provide seamless hospitality services and bookings, ensuring unparalleled comfort and efficiency.

With a strong foundation in hospitality and a passion for modern design, we deliver high-quality accommodations that cater to the refined tastes of our clients. Our portfolio includes exquisite boutique hotels, upscale villas, and residential complexes, alongside strategic partnerships with leading developers and consulting firms.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the holiday home and hospitality experience across the Middle East, setting new standards for luxury, comfort, and innovation through a comprehensive, app-based solution for all our operations.

Our Mission

To develop advanced hotels and boutique properties that leverage the latest technology for smart, seamless services, making the booking process for holiday homes and hotel apartments an effortless and accessible experience.

Meet Our Founder

Ahmed El-Deeb

The mastermind behind the unique ELEGANT international hospitality brand, Ahmed is distinguished by his dynamic approach to work and talent for launching and nurturing new concepts. After receiving his Bachelor's in Engineering from Southern Illinois University and Master's from the University of Alabama, he honed his skills in project management in Los Angeles.

Returning to Egypt, he dedicated himself to developing Elegant Hospitality, with the primary goal of revolutionizing the region's hospitality industry by leveraging technology to streamline operations and deliver a seamless customer experience.

Our Expert Team
